Why should you buy insurance?

  1. It's affordable. There are new and affordable insurance options for people and families without insurance.
  2. It's easy and simple. You can compare coverage levels, costs, and quality scores between plans - online, in person, or on the phone.
  3. You get complete care. All insurance plans will have to cover doctor's visits, hospitalizations, ER care, maternity care, and prescriptions.
  4. Financial help is available. Assistance will be available to you and your family find a plan that fits your budget.
  5. You deserve peace of mind. You never know when a medical emergency will strike, and unanticipated expenses can be financially devastating. You can protect you health and your family's future by getting health insurance.
